Sage Sarvie is a multidisciplinary designer from Ottawa, Canada. She studied architectural conservation & sustainability engineering at Carleton University and has industry experience in Ottawa, Irvine, New York, and Seattle. In addition, Sarvie has an MBA from Ivey Business School, where she has been awarded two scholarships, won pitch competitions, and received an entrepreneurial certificate.
